About this project

This is a portfolio demo by Rajani.

Meridian Realty is a fictional brokerage built to showcase an AI-powered lead triage system. Submit the form to see it in action — within seconds, an AI agent will text you to qualify your needs, score your intent, and offer to book a showing.

  • Form submission triggers a Make automation
  • OpenAI GPT-4o runs the qualifying conversation
  • Twilio handles SMS / WhatsApp messaging
  • Cal.com books the showing automatically
  • Slack alerts the agent for hot leads

Built by Rajani at The AI Internship — exploring how AI replaces the inside sales desk for service businesses.
